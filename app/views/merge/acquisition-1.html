Merge organisations

Use this service if you need to apply to merge two existing organisations in HMCTS.

Merging HMCTS organisations could respond to two different business processes:

  • Acquiring an existing organisation
  • Merging two existing organisation

In both cases, the resulting process will end with one organisation holding all the users and cases, and deleting the remaining organisations.

Acquiring process

This process involve one organisation acquiring another organisation, both existing in HMCTS, so the acquired organisation will transfer all their users and cases to the acquiring organisation in HMCTS , so the acquired organisation can be deleted from HMCTS. The acquiring organisationb will have all the users and cases from the deleted organisation.

Merging process

This process involve two existing organisations at HMCTS joining operations, that means that, a new organisation will be created in HMCTS so both initial organisations will transfer their users and cases to the new organisation, so they can be deleted from HMCTS. The new organisation will have all the users and cases from the deleted organisations.
